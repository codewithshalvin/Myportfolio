🌟 About Me – Shalvin Felcia

Passionate creator · CS student · Small business owner

Hey there! I'm Shalvin Felcia — a passionate creator, a computer science student, and a proud small business owner who loves bringing imagination to life through both code and craft.

I've always been drawn to the magic of making — whether it's designing a beautiful illusion painting, pouring resin into stunning forms, or writing lines of code that turn ideas into working apps. My journey blends the worlds of technology and art, where I get to experiment, build, and constantly learn.

🎨 The Creative Hustle

What started as a hobby turned into a full-blown small business — from resin art and illusion paintings to hoop embroidery and handcrafted shadow boxes. Each piece is crafted with passion, patience, and purpose.

I recently stepped into the world of YouTube to share my creations, tutorials, and behind-the-scenes processes with the world. It's been an exciting way to connect with fellow creatives and inspire others to try something new.

💻 The Tech Explorer

As a computer science student, from a young age I have always been curious about how technology works. This curiosity gradually turned into a strong passion for Computer Science and Engineering. The idea that we can create software, websites, and applications that solve real-world problems fascinates me.

I have worked on several small projects such as a hotel table booking frontend, a calculator program, and a Towers of Hanoi game. Currently, I am also interested in modern web development and building systems that allow users to customize products online.

For me, Computer Science and Engineering is not just a course of study but a journey of creativity, innovation, and continuous learning. I believe that with dedication and curiosity, I can use technology to create useful solutions and contribute to the digital world.

💫 Beyond the Work

I'm also a proud daughter, a dreamer, and a doer who believes in growing, learning, and giving back. From college events to time with family — I stay grounded and grateful.

Got a cool collab idea? Let's connect over art, tech, or coffee!
